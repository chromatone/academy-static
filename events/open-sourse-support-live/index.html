Here's a big live performance of all my upcoming op-z tracks. It's going to be my new album called 'open source support'.

I don't want to dive too deep into music production for polishing it now. The more layers I build, the more elaborated are the tracks, the more far I get away from the idea of live media generation. I've got the vibes, got the visuals and got the mood to play them all at once.

Today it was just a jam with two cameras, but for me it was just the first of my new concert tour with the album. So... hope I'll find some new places to perform this program again and again, polishing the set to be someday recorded for the actual album release.

So for now you can feel the live improvisation vibes, hear the imperfections and just have a nice time with my music. The title 'open source support' is about me thinking about the future of my open source projects.

Yesterday I've created a Ko-fi account, it's service for collecting donations and selling nice things to people worldwide. I imagine establishing a nice feedback loop, when my open and free efforts are appreciated. Like every once in a while people stumble upon my open source creations and express gratitude with donations, making me stay on track of my unleashed creativity. May be it's just a dream. But with you it definitely can become a reality!
