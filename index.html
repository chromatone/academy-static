Welcome to Chromatone Academy! ​

This is a growing educational platform for learning and practicing music using our eyes. We are currently working in 4 main directions, forming 4 major educational programs. Each of them includes a number of research and design projects, that bring the experience to be shared in available materials, courses and educational sessions both online and offline. Stay tuned for more!

Educational programs ​

Visual Music Theory
4 projects
Deep dive into music theory, fostering the development of original composition skills. Graduates emerge as Composers, wielding a profound understanding of music's structure and principles.
Collaborative Performance
2 projects
Practical application of music skills within a community setting. Graduates, known as Conductors, are prepared to guide and lead musical ensembles in a collaborative environment.
Web Development
3 projects
Using completely open source stack to create 100% open source apps as gifts by the generous community of passionate web developers for broad audience of music explorers
Live Multimedia Production
3 projects
Creating multimedia representations of musical explorations
