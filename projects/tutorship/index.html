Chromatone Tutorship
Personal guidance for independent students to better engage with all the accessible knowledge and tooling for visual music exploration.

We're soon starting our online guidance program for those, who want to study and then teach others the Visual Music Language.

It's the current state introduction to music theory for beginner composers, who need a tool to get well with all the vastness of music theory phenomena and concepts. We're not talking much about practical aspects as we an construct tools to match any level of musical ability. The main focus is on enabling a whole new generation of casual musicians, who are very easy going with quite complex theory and not biased by any particular nation culture.

It's new, quite scientific way to see music. And it's best working for those just starting music explorations. It's very convenient to have all these tools and features for going your own unique way through music. But at some moment music will make you close your eyes and enjoy it in full. Yet visual music is a natural development of Isaac Newton's ideas and it can become a new feature for societies worldwide. An ability to get together and freely compose music with people of all musical knowledge levels and backgrounds.

Playing music together is a very deep experience, connecting people somehow very closely. It's the delight of souls touching at some particular frequencies and in sync. And we are sharing it to be shared further! After a student masters all modules they get an independently verifiable certificate of graduation. Graduates will be able to publish links to their multimedia educational content and apply for Tutor role in Chromatone Academy.
