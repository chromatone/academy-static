Chromatone Tutorship
Group guidance for independent students to better engage with all the accessible knowledge and tooling for visual music exploration.

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our online tutorship program, Chromatone. This program is designed for those who wish to explore and master the Visual Music Language, and then share this knowledge by teaching others.

Chromatone offers a fresh, scientific approach to music theory, making it an ideal starting point for beginner composers. Our program provides a comprehensive introduction to music theory, with a focus on fostering a new generation of musicians who can navigate complex theory with ease and without cultural bias.

As part of the program, students will be invited to join a private Discord server. This online community offers weekly group video calls, daily text feedback, and access to a growing network of music students and researchers. It’s a space where you can chart your unique musical journey, with all the tools and features you need at your fingertips.

Visual music, a concept that builds upon Isaac Newton’s ideas, has the potential to become a transformative societal feature. It enables people of all musical knowledge levels and backgrounds to come together and compose music freely. The experience of playing music together creates deep connections, as souls touch at particular frequencies and in sync.

Upon completion of all modules, students will receive an independently verifiable certificate of graduation. Graduates will have the opportunity to publish links to their multimedia educational content and apply for a Tutor role within the Chromatone Academy.

Join us in this exciting journey and become part of the first group of Chromatone students. Let’s explore the delightful intersection of sound and color together!
